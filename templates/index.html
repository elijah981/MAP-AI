Welcome to the Self-Discovery Tool

This tool is designed to help you identify your unique Motivated Abilities Pattern® (MAP®) based on the System for Identifying Motivated Abilities (SIMA®) by Arthur Miller Jr. and Bill Burns.

Main Goals

  • Identify your unique abilities and preferences.
  • Understand the activities that you find most satisfying.
  • Discover the subject matter, circumstances, and relationships that motivate you.

How It Works

We guide you through a series of steps, each aimed at helping you identify different aspects of your MAP. Your inputs will be analyzed using advanced AI algorithms to provide you with insights into your motivated abilities, subject matter, and more.
